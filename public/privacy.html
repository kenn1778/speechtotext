Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

SpeechWeb ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our speech-to-text application.

Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign in (via email or Google), we receive your email address and display name.

Audio recordings: Audio you record is temporarily uploaded to AWS S3 for transcription via OpenAI Whisper. Audio files are deleted after processing.

Transcripts: Generated transcripts are stored locally in your browser. No transcript data is sent to our servers except during the transcription process.

How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely for authentication. Audio is only processed to generate transcripts. We do not sell, share, or use your data for training or advertising.

Data Storage

Authentication is handled by AWS Cognito. Audio files are stored temporarily in AWS S3 (us-east-1). The app has no persistent database — history is stored in your browser's localStorage.

Third-Party Services

  • AWS Cognito — authentication
  • AWS S3 / Lambda / API Gateway — audio upload & transcription
  • OpenAI Whisper — speech-to-text processing
  • Google — optional social sign-in
